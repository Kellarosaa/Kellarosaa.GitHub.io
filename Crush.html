About Candy Crush

Candy Crush is an engaging puzzle game that includes a social element. Although it is a puzzle game, it does not make it difficult for you with difficult inferences. On the contrary, the game naturally leads you to find the answer, making everyone love the colorful candy world with countless levels in this game.
The game is candy themed, your task is to eliminate candies in rows of three to five. To get a higher score in the game, you need to put together candies of the same type and explode, the effect will be different. The obstacles in the game will be unlocked in turn and every 15 levels you will overcome. The first 20 obstacles will be unlocked directly, however, it requires a train ticket (sometimes a train ticket, or a plane ticket) after 35 levels.

Control?

Use the mouse to change the position of the candy.
